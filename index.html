How AI Powers Your Experience

Ticketmaster uses AI to personalise your experience. Here's how:

Event Recommendations

We suggest events based on your purchase history, browsing activity, and genre preferences. Each recommendation explains why it was chosen.

Fair Price Indicator

Our AI compares ticket prices against similar events at the same venue to give you context on value — Great Value, Fair Price, or Premium.

ARIA Assistant

ARIA uses AI to understand your questions and provide instant help. A human agent is always available if you prefer.

Your Control

You can manage your preferences, privacy settings, and data at any time in Settings → AI & Privacy. We only personalise what you ask us to.
